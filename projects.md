## IndaPlants Experiment
A project involving autonomous holonomic robots designed to optimize plant life through coordinated movement.

**Technologies Used**: C++, Arduino, XBee S2 modules, A* pathfinding.

## Retinal Detachment Modeling
Research on fracture mechanics in ophthalmic biomechanics, focusing on retinal detachment under low-velocity blunt force impact.

**Technologies Used**: MATLAB, FEA, Griffith fracture criterion.
